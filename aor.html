Air, Ocean & Road

Ddoy groups form a safe network of procurement and delivery from and to any part of the world.

Air freight: We are on collaboration with all of the major courier and air cargo transporters and provide a door to door consignment delivery along withrd customs clearance service.
Ocean freight: We work with all major marine shipping companies to offer sea freight delivery to a port, and onward transport by road to the final destination. This could be for full containers or partial containers (LCL - Less than a Container Load).
Road freight: Our company can provide cargo transport via road either by groupage or for full containers, including inter-continental delivery by road.
